About This Project

This website is part of a graduation project for the Vocational Certificate level from a reputable educational institution. It has been developed to demonstrate the knowledge, abilities, and skills acquired throughout the course of study.

Asian Maritime Technological College.

This project is supported and carried out under the supervision of the Asian Maritime Technological College., a leading maritime educational institution renowned for its expertise in maritime technology, simulation-based training, and the development of skilled professionals to meet the needs of the global shipping, logistics, and maritime industries.

Note: This website has been developed for educational purposes and solely as part of the learning assessment for the Vocational Certificate program.

Project Objectives

• Enhance English vocabulary learning and retention through the use of mnemonic techniques
• Develop English language skills in reading, writing, and vocabulary memorization
• Apply mnemonic techniques to support future language learning and academic development
• Create a learning project that can be presented as a model or example to educational institutions
