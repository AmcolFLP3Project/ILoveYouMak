📚 English Grammar Mnemonics

Learn Parts of Speech and the 12 Tenses with easy-to-remember mnemonic techniques that make English grammar simple

🎭 Parts of Speech

Noun - Pronoun - Adjective - Preposition - Conjunction - Interjection - Adverb - Preposition
1. Noun (คำนาม)
คำที่ใช้เรียกชื่อคน สัตว์ สิ่งของ สถานที่
"Names of things we can touch or think"
Example: cat, book, Bangkok, happiness
2. Pronoun (คำสรรพนาม)
คำที่ใช้แทนคำนาม
"Pro = Professional substitute for nouns"
Example: I, you, he, she, it, they, we
3. Adjective (คำคุณศัพท์)
คำที่ขยายคำนามหรือคำสรรพนาม
"Add beauty to nouns"
Example: beautiful, big, smart, red
4. Verb (คำกริยา)
คำที่แสดงการกระทำหรือสถานะ
"Very important action words"
Example: run, eat, sleep, is, am, are
5. Adverb (คำกริยาวิเศษณ์)
คำที่ขยายคำกริยา คำคุณศัพท์ หรือคำกริยาวิเศษณ์อื่น
"Add verb power (often ends with -ly)"
Example: quickly, very, well, often
6. Preposition (คำบุพบท)
คำที่แสดงความสัมพันธ์ระหว่างคำ
"Pre-position = Before position"
Example: in, on, at, under, over
7. Conjunction (คำสันธาน)
คำที่เชื่อมคำ วลี หรือประโยค
"Connects like a junction"
Example: and, but, or, because, if
8. Interjection (คำอุทาน)
คำที่แสดงอารมณ์หรือความรู้สึก
"Interrupt with emotion!"
Example: Oh! Wow! Ouch! Hurray!

⏰ 12 Tenses (12 กาล)

1. Present Simple
S + V1 (s/es)
"Simple Present = Simple Life"
ใช้กับความจริง นิสัย กิจวัตร
I eat rice every day.
I drink beer every day.
2. Present Continuous
S + am/is/are + V-ing
"Continuing NOW"
กำลังทำในขณะนี้
I am eating rice now.
I am drinking beer now.
3. Present Perfect
S + have/has + V3
"Perfect = Finished but connected to now"
เสร็จแล้วแต่เกี่ยวข้องกับปัจจุบัน
I have eaten rice.
I have drunk beer.
4. Present Perfect Continuous
S + have/has + been + V-ing
"Perfect Continuing = Started past, continuing now"
เริ่มในอดีต ยังคงดำเนินต่อ
I have been eating rice for 10 minutes.
I have been drinking beer for 10 minutes.
5. Past Simple
S + V2
"Simple Past = Simple Finished"
เหตุการณ์ที่เสร็จสิ้นในอดีต
I ate rice yesterday.
I drank beer yesterday.
6. Past Continuous
S + was/were + V-ing
"Was/Were Continuing in past"
กำลังทำในอดีต
I was eating rice when you called.
I was drinking beer when you called.
7. Past Perfect
S + had + V3
"Had Perfect = Earlier past action"
เหตุการณ์ที่เกิดก่อนในอดีต
I had eaten rice before you came.
I had drunk beer before you came.
8. Past Perfect Continuous
S + had + been + V-ing
"Had been continuing before another past action"
กำลังทำมาก่อนเหตุการณ์อื่นในอดีต
I had been eating rice for an hour before you came.
I had been drinking beer for an hour before you came.
9. Future Simple
S + will + V1
"Will = Future intention"
จะทำในอนาคต
I will eat rice tomorrow.
I will drink beer tomorrow.
10. Future Continuous
S + will + be + V-ing
"Will be continuing in future"
จะกำลังทำในอนาคต
I will be eating rice at 7 PM tomorrow.
I will be drinking beer at 8 PM tomorrow.
11. Future Perfect
S + will + have + V3
"Will have perfect = Completed by future time"
จะเสร็จก่อนเวลาในอนาคต
I will have eaten rice by 8 PM.
I will have drunk beer by 9 PM.
12. Future Perfect Continuous
S + will + have + been + V-ing
"Will have been continuing = Duration until future time"
จะกำลังทำมาเป็นระยะเวลา
I will have been eating rice for 30 minutes by the time you arrive.
I will have been drinking beer for 30 minutes by the time you arrive.
