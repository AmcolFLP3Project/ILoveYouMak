Rhyme Patterns

ABAB Pattern

Line 1 rhymes with Line 3, Line 2 rhymes with Line 4

"The cat sat on the mat (A)
While birds flew in the sky (B)
It wore a little hat (A)
And watched the clouds go by (B)"

AABB Pattern

Lines 1&2 rhyme, Lines 3&4 rhyme (couplets)

"Roses are red (A)
Violets are blue (B)
Learning is fun (C)
And memory's true (B)"

Acrostic Method

First letters spell out the target information

"Read Our Yearbook
Good Boys Invite Visitors
(Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet)"
